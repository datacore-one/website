Why Datacore?

For those who write down more tasks than they finish.

⚙

AI That Does, Not Tracks

Project management apps track tasks. Datacore executes them. Your todo list becomes a delegation interface to AI agents that work while you rest.

🔒

Open Source, Local-First

Your extended mind shouldn't have a landlord. Your data on your machine, in plain text formats that you own forever.

🕑

Future-Proof Architecture

Built on plain text and open standards that outlive any AI provider. Your data works with whatever AI wins tomorrow.

🧠

Your AI Finally Remembers

Insights from every AI conversation are captured automatically. Chatbots forget. Datacore doesn't. Context compounds over time.

🚀

Agents That Work 24/7

Research, writing, analysis, project tracking. Tag a task, and AI agents handle it while you live your life.

🌐

Modular & Extensible

Start with the core. Add modules for trading, content, research. Build your own. The community contributes.
